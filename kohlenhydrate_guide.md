## English Version

### What are Carbohydrates?

Think of carbohydrates like tiny energy building blocks! They're like fuel for a car - without them, your body can't work properly. You can find carbohydrates in many foods like bread, pasta, fruits, and even in milk!

---

### The Sugar Family

Sugars belong to the big family of carbohydrates. There are different types of sugars, just like there are different members in a family.

#### Simple Sugars (Monosaccharides) - The Loners

These are the smallest sugar building blocks. They consist of just one sugar molecule.

| Name | Where do you find it? | Did you know? |
|------|----------------------|---------------|
| **Glucose** (Grape sugar) | Grapes, honey, candies | This is the most important energy source for your brain! |
| **Fructose** (Fruit sugar) | Apples, pears, honey | It's the sweetest natural sugar |
| **Galactose** | Part of milk | Your body makes it from lactose |

#### Double Sugars (Disaccharides) - The Pairs

These sugars are made of two simple sugars holding hands.

| Name | What's it made of? | Where do you find it? |
|------|-------------------|----------------------|
| **Sucrose** (Table sugar) | Glucose + Fructose | Sugar bowl at home, cakes, cookies |
| **Lactose** (Milk sugar) | Glucose + Galactose | Milk, yogurt, cheese |
| **Maltose** (Malt sugar) | Glucose + Glucose | Malt candies, when baking bread |

#### Complex Sugars (Polysaccharides) - The Big Groups

These are long chains of many simple sugars - like a pearl necklace!

| Name | What is it? | Where do you find it? |
|------|------------|----------------------|
| **Starch** | Energy storage for plants | Potatoes, rice, bread, pasta |
| **Glycogen** | Energy storage in your body | In your muscles and liver |
| **Cellulose** | Makes plants strong | Vegetables, fruits (fiber) |

---

### Lactose - The Milk Sugar

Lactose is the sugar found in milk from cows, sheep, and even from your mom when you were a baby!

**How does it work?**
1. You drink milk
2. In your tummy, there's a little helper called "lactase"
3. Lactase cuts lactose into two pieces: glucose and galactose
4. Your body can then use these small pieces for energy

**Lactose Intolerance:** Some people don't have enough lactase. When they drink milk, they get tummy aches. For them, there's lactose-free milk!

---

### Fructose - The Fruit Sugar

Fructose is what makes fruit taste so yummy and sweet!

**Where is there lots of fructose?**
- Apples
- Pears
- Grapes
- Honey
- Fruit juices

**Good to know:** Fruit is healthy even though it contains fructose! That's because fruit also has vitamins, water, and fiber. But too much fruit juice or sweets with lots of fructose aren't so good for your teeth.

---

### How Does Your Body Digest Sugar?

```
Food â†’ Mouth (Chewing) â†’ Stomach â†’ Small Intestine â†’ Blood â†’ Energy!
```

1. **In your mouth:** Your saliva already starts breaking down starch into smaller pieces
2. **In your stomach:** The food gets broken down more
3. **In your small intestine:** This is where the magic happens! Enzymes (tiny helpers) cut the sugars into simple sugars
4. **Into your blood:** The simple sugars go into your blood
5. **Energy:** Your cells use the sugar as energy!
